About

Welcome to Brain Champion Academy - a leading educational institute that caters to the needs of the next generation. We specialize in identifying basic learning problems in children and developing customized programs to help them overcome these obstacles.

As a franchise of Mastermind Abacus, a well-respected education institute based in Trivandrum, Kerala, India, we are committed to providing our students with the highest quality education possible. Our programs are designed to help children develop a strong foundation in math and improve their mental arithmetic skills.

BENEFITS OF ABACUS.

Our unique game-based approach to mental math training helps develop dynamic number sense in children, while keeping them engaged and interested.

With our program, children can overcome the fear of math right from an early age, setting them up for success in the future.

Classes

Learning Abacus, Drawing, and Carnatic Music offer several benefits for children. These skills help them to develop their cognitive and creative abilities, emotional well-being, and overall academic performance. As a result, investing in these programs is a wise decision for parents looking to support their child's growth and success.

Abacus MasterMind Program

Abacus training helps children to develop a strong foundation in math and improves their mental arithmetic skills. It also enhances concentration and memory, which can be transferred to other areas of life, including academics. The ability to perform mental calculations also boosts confidence and helps children to overcome the fear of math.

Drawing and Painting Classes

Drawing classes help children to express themselves and develop their creativity, which is a valuable skill in both personal and professional life. Drawing also improves hand-eye coordination and fine motor skills, promotes problem-solving, and enhances focus and concentration. Additionally, drawing can be a relaxing and therapeutic activity for children, reducing stress and anxiety.

Carnatic Music

Carnatic music classes help children to appreciate and understand music, develop a sense of rhythm, and enhance their auditory skills. Moreover, music has been shown to enhance cognitive abilities, including memory and language skills.
